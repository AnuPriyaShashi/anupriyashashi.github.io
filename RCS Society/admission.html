Thank you for your interest in RCP Public School. The Admissions Application is now available for incoming freshman. Admission is by an exam open to all eligible students in Kishanganz. Our hope is that your curriculum will inspire you to develop your intellectual passions, not suffer from unnecessary stress. Taken full advantage of the opportunities available to you in school and achieved at a high level.

Application form is available interested candidate can take it from the school’s office. Procedure of admission will be student applying for Nursery class has to go through the oral interview, and LKG and UKG class students have to give entrance exam and then oral interview will be there. For Std.01 to Std.06 students will have to give entrance exam and will get admission on the school on the basis of last school exam marks.
