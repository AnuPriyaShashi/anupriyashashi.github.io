RCS Public School Overview
RCS Society have introduced the RCS Public School in 2001 for the development of children's education along with exploration of individual brilliance. We believe that the inception of the children's education is the way of association and guidance among the parents and the teachers. For this purpose we need the trust and faith of parents to our organization so that we can develop a health and prospers environment for your children’s. Here education will not be just academics but it goes beyond books and classrooms to ensure all round development of a student's personality, so as to lead the students to gain awareness of their attitudes, strength, weakness, potential, self-motivation, habits and values of their life orientation. The goal is not just acquiring knowledge but that which is experimental, value-skill-action oriented and related to life to transform our students into enlightened citizens.
