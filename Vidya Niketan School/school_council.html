Vidya Niketan School Council is committed to supporting success for all our students to achieve their potential in a safe, positive, inclusive and caring environment that not only enhances the quality of our children's education, but their social and emotional well-being.

We are a group of parents that meet with the Principal and a teacher representative approximately 6 times a year to discuss school activities, fundraising, and other issues affecting our school community.

The school is always in need, so our fundraising is key to purchasing itemsnot covered by the board. We thank you for supporting our various fundraisers. Last year our fundraising efforts allowed us to purchase:

- 7 Smartboards for classrooms
- Equipment for the kindergarten outdoor area
- Tents for outdoor sporting events
- The "greening" of the school
- Food for the Healthy Food Basket program
- A music sound system
We are always looking for parent volunteers for fundraising events such as Fun Day, held in early June each year. Your help in any way would be greatly appreciated. If you are interested, please leave your name at the office or email Jana and a council member will contact you.
