Vidya Niketan - Patna

Vidya Niketan School promotes excellent education along with exploration of individual brilliance. We, at Vidya Niketan opened this school to vigorously and proactively seek the voice of our students and parents towards meeting their dynamic eduational needs enthusiastically.We shall inculcate and ensure qualitative change in the perosonality of the students by cultivating best human behaviour, habits, skills, and nourishment of preventative capability through professional excellence in academics and extra-curriculam activities.

We shall create a vibrant environment and continuosly improve upon the infrastructure and necesary services sensing contemporay needs through implementation of quality management system of international standard. We shall recognize and respect that positive attitudes firmly influence in promoting the harmonious development and true dignity of all young people who are under our domain.

We shall apply modern academic principles in the management system of the school that result in high quality of education.
