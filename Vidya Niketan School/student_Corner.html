Code Of Conduct

1.To inculcate the willingness and likeness for using English language, and also to enhance the student's English speaking skills,
conversation only in English has been made mandatory for all students.
2.Misbehaviour inside the school campus and during bus journey is a punishable offence.
3.Parents and students shall not give to the teachers any gifts in whatsoever form.
4.Once inside the campus, the responsibilty for taking care of one's belongings lies with the students.
6.Studnts suffering from contagious diseases shall not be allowed to attend classes until the student furnishes fitness certificater from a doctor.
7.Students should come fully prepared to take up the scheduled examinations. Cheating or copying in any form will lead to thevexpulsion of the student.
8.Mobiles, CDs, PDs, Cameras, MP3 Players and any other such electronic or mechanical gadgets / devices are not allowed in the school. If caught, such articles will be destroyed by the school authorities.
9.Students are expected to take utmost care of school's property as their own property.
10.Students are required to maintain the code of conduct strictly when inside the school campus and during the bus journey.
