Campus Life

Campus life at Vidya Niketan is as varied and engaging as the remarkable students who go here. We're athletes, artists, activists, hikers, singers, researchers and adventurers, and We knows how to make us happy.
Let's start with our student clubs and organizations—nearly 400 at last count—where we do our a cappella thing, review movies for the campus newspaper and organize medical outreach trips. We believe in Vidya Niketan mission to make the world a better place, so we dive headfirst into service. Not just in Bihar, but across the globe.
We promote sustainability through faculty/student research, student internships, innovative community partnerships and widespread volunteerism. Vidya Niketan is producing leaders who understand both the challenge and the opportunity in a sustainable future.
Nothing gets us going like some friendly competition, and Vidya Niketan incredible sports and recreation program has the top-notch facilities and endless choices— dance—to mash our inner couch potato.
